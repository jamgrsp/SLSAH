EASTER RECOLLECTION (AY 2020-2021)

Saint Louis School of Aurora Hill, Inc. together with Don Bosco School of Baguio City, Inc. celebrated their Easter Recollection at Saint Bernadette Soubirous chapel facilitated by Rev. Fr. Benedict Villapa and Rev. Fr. Elpidio Silug. It was indeed a meaningful and memorable recollection for the teachers and staff of both schools since they were able to enter the Jubilee door, receive the sacrament of confession and celebrate the Holy Eucharist at Immaculate Conception Parish (Acop) which was presided by the newly ordained priest Rev. Fr. Julius Cesar Panilo.
